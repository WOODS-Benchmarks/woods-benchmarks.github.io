Updates

WOODS is still under development. We are always looking for feedback and contributions. In the near future, we will be working towards adding more datasets from real world problems, including sequential and temporal data from new modalities.


Initial Release! (Version 0.1)

March 20th 2022

Beta version of WOODS is released to the public!

Includes the following benchmarks:

  • Spurious-Fourier: Spurious features encoded in the frequency domain

  • Temporal Colored MNIST with Source-domains

  • Temporal Colored MNIST with Time-domains

  • CAP: Sleep classification across different machines

  • SEDFx: Sleep Classification across age groups

  • PCL: Motor imagery classification across data-gathering procedures

  • LSA64: Sign language video classification across speakers

  • HHAR: Human activity recognition across smart devices

With the following OOD generalization objectives:

  • Empirical Risk Minimization (ERM)

  • Invariant Risk Minimization (IRM)

  • Variance Risk Extrapolation (VREx)

  • Spectral Decoupling (SD)

  • Information Bottleneck Empirical Risk Minimization (IB-ERM)

Stay tuned for more updates! More datasets and OOD generalization objectives will be added soon!
